The Stylist

Meet Heather.

With a career built on passion, precision, and genuine connection, Heather Slonski has been transforming hair — and turning heads — in the Grass Valley area for over two decades. She has a rare gift for truly listening, then delivering exactly what you envisioned (and sometimes better than you imagined).

Whether she's painting a sun-kissed balayage, creating dimensional color from root to tip, or crafting the perfect cut for your face shape and lifestyle, Heather brings artistry and warmth to every chair. Her clients don't just leave looking beautiful — they leave feeling like themselves, elevated.

Sit in her chair once and you'll feel it — a genuine connection that goes beyond the appointment. Hair is how Heather tells your story. And once she knows yours, she never forgets it.
